Part1
Plant A is big and plant B is small.
植物 A 很大，植物 B 很小。

Plant A is thick and plant B is thin.
植物 A 很粗，植物 B 很細。

Plant A has a small flower and plant B has a big flower.
植物 A 有一朵小花，植物 B 有一朵大花。

Plant A has a thick stem and plant B has a thin stem.
植物 A 有粗粗的莖，植物 B 有細細的莖。

Plant A has spikes and plant B has leaves.
植物 A 有刺，植物 B 有葉子。


Part2
The chimp has a belt like a crown.
黑猩猩戴著一條像皇冠一樣的腰帶。

The frog sat next to one little star.
青蛙坐在一顆小星星旁邊。

Sam and Kit have got some sticks.
Sam 和 Kit 拿著一些樹枝。

They are stuck out in a storm.
他們困在暴風雨中。

The flag is high on the mast.
旗子高高掛在桅杆上。


Part3
The carrots are orange.
紅蘿蔔是橘色的。

The carrots are long.
紅蘿蔔是長長的。

They are fresh.
它們很新鮮。

They are sweet and tasty.
它們又甜又好吃。

The carrots are orange and long.
紅蘿蔔是橘色又長長的。

Carrots are healthy vegetables.
紅蘿蔔是健康的蔬菜。

Carrots are healthy vegetables, and they are good for us.
紅蘿蔔是健康的蔬菜，而且對我們有益。



Part4
CCCVC ：前面 3 個子音 + 中間 1 個母音 + 最後 1 個子音 
scrub = scr / u / b → 刷子

split = spl / i / t → 分開

strip = str / i / p → 長條

splat = spl / a / t → 潑濺聲

splash = spl / a / sh → 水花濺（灑）出
